About

Kausthoub Vishnu is an accomplished alto saxophonist with more than 8 years of experience. While rooted in jazz music, he is open to perform other styles if needed. Inspired by the guidance of his teachers and mentors over the years, he aspires to emulate their mastery and to leave an impact on his music. With a fervent dedication to lifelong learning and artistic growth, Kausthoub Vishnu looks forward in his pursuit, fueled by his passion and determination.
